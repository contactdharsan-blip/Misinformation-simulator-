### 1. Network Structure Effects
- **Homophily strength**: How similar agents connect

### 2. Temporal Dynamics

### 3. Intervention Mechanisms

### 4. Demographic and Psychological Factors

- **Education correlation**: How education affects susceptibility
- **Trust levels**: Institutional trust variations
- **Emotional triggers**: Fear, anger, hope effects
